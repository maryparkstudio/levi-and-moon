The Person Behind It All

Hi, I'm Mary 🌸

I'm a mom who fell completely in love with capturing every precious moment with my baby, Levi. From the very first days, I found myself pouring everything into making every milestone feel as special as they truly are — because I know how fast it all goes.

As a graphic designer, photographer, and illustrator, I've spent years honing my eye for branding and detail. But it was becoming a mom that gave all of those skills a deeper purpose. I started Levi & Moon to channel everything I love — storytelling and creating experiences people will remember forever.

Every family deserves to feel celebrated. Every milestone deserves to be honored beautifully. That's not just my business — it's my dream.
